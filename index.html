FAQ

Because we ask them all the time

How do we get FREE access to GCGA Sec+ Resources?

Using your personal email, contact Denise.Racz@us.af.mil with the following information:
Rank & Full Name
AFSC (i.e. 3D0X2)
Military Status: Active Duty, Guard, or Reserve
IT Fundamentals (ITF) Start Date (Determines your Sec+ Version: SY0-501 or SY0-601 of GCGA)
More information can be found HERE.

How do we sign up for the Sec+ Pre-Test?

Pre-test signups are every Tuesday at 0900 in Room 318 of Thomson Hall.
NPS students must bring a 341 signed by their current instructor, and if applicable, your MTL. You will also need to send an email to: 336TRS.UUB.DoD8570Program@us.af.mil.

More information can be found HERE.

Can I bring a BX/Commissary Cart to the Dorms? Do I have to Return it?

Yes. You may bring a cart from the Main BX, Mini BX (Shopette), and Commissary to the dorms.
However,
you  must return the BX cart to either the Main BX, or Mini BX. You MUST return the Commissary cart back to the Commissary. The BX and Commissary are two different companies.
